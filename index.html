About

I am a Post-Doctoral Researcher in the Health Informatics Lab (LIMe) group at UCLouvain, affiliated with the ICTEAM institute. I recently completed my PhD in Computer Science & Engineering under the supervision of Prof. Etienne Rivière.

My research focuses on resource management in distributed stream processing — from fine-grain CPU/memory elasticity and multi-query task placement to proactive capacity planning for production data pipelines built on Apache Kafka and Apache Flink.

I am currently working as a post-doctoral researcher in the LIMe Lab under the supervision of Prof. Sébastien Jodogne, where I am applying my expertise as a software engineer and devops practitioner to the design and integration of medical standards and tools for open-sourced clinical data platforms such as OpenMRS.

Prior to academia, I co-founded Read&Rate (2017–2021), a publishing-sector startup where I served as CTO, leading all technical and management aspects of the company.
